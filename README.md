Control your Ohme EV charger directly from [Homey](https://homey.app). Monitor charging status, power usage, and battery level in real time. Switch between smart charging, max power, and paused modes without opening the Ohme app.

This is an unofficial community app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or related to Ohme Technologies Limited.

## Supported Chargers

- Ohme ePod
- Ohme Home Pro
- Ohme Home (legacy)
- Ohme Go (legacy)

## Features

- Real-time charging status, power, current, and voltage monitoring
- Vehicle battery level and energy meter tracking in Homey's Energy tab
- Charge mode control (smart charge, max charge, pause)
- Target charge percentage and departure time
- Preconditioning duration control
- Flow cards for status changes, charge approval, vehicle selection, and price cap
- Button lock, price cap, approval, and sleep mode settings
- Works on both Homey Pro and Homey Cloud
